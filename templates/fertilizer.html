Fertilizer Recommendations Based on NPK Values

Loamy Soil - Recommended Fertilizer

For loamy soil, a balanced fertilizer with equal parts of Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium is ideal. Example: NPK 10-10-10.

Sandy Soil - Recommended Fertilizer

Sandy soil requires fertilizers with more Potassium and Phosphorus to retain nutrients. Consider using NPK 10-20-10.

Clay Soil - Recommended Fertilizer

For clay soil, fertilizers with higher Nitrogen content work best. Try using NPK 20-10-10.
